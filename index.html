Sahel AI is an AI risk management company. We help organizations safely navigate the deployment of artificial intelligence by operationalizing AI risk management protocols, ensuring systems are secure, compliant, and reliable.

Solutions

As enterprise AI adoption accelerates, the gap between AI capabilities and organizational safety guardrails widens. We partner with organizations to build and execute robust frameworks for AI governance. Our core focus is translating theoretical AI safety principles into operational reality, helping teams map vulnerabilities, mitigate risks, and deploy models with confidence.

Research

Technical AI governance requires a deep understanding of model behaviors. We conduct and publish research in AI safety to advance the scientific community's understanding of systemic risks.
